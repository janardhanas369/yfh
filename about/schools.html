On the Ground

Where We Show Up Every Week

Our coaches visit each partner school on a structured weekly schedule, delivering coaching sessions, fitness assessments, and match preparation directly on the school ground. No child needs to travel, pay a fee, or own equipment to take part.

Enrol Your School

Government school principals are warmly invited to bring Yes For Hockey! to their students. We provide the coaches and the structure, at no cost to the school or its children.
