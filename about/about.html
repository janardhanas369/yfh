Who We Are

An NGO Built on Belief

YES For Hockey began with a simple belief — that a child's address should never decide whether they get to play, learn, or dream of a national stage. We work inside government schools, where resources are thin but talent is abundant, and we provide what is missing: certified coaching, free equipment, and steady academic support.

From a single school visit in 2019 to a network of 40+ schools across three states today, every step has been powered by volunteers, donors, and educators who refused to look away.

Our Commitment

Every program is completely FREE for the children. No child is ever charged for coaching, equipment, or support. Non-negotiable.
