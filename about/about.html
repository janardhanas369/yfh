Who We Are

Reviving Grassroots Hockey, One School at a Time

Yes For Hockey! Association (YFHA) is a non-profit committed to reviving and strengthening field hockey at the grassroots level across India, starting with government schools in Bengaluru. We identify talented children, deliver professional coaching, structured training, fitness programs, and skill development, and build competitions and pathways toward district, state, national, and international opportunities.

Our Vision

To become India's leading grassroots hockey development organisation, empowering every child with the opportunity to learn, compete, and achieve excellence.

Our Mission

  • Discovering and nurturing young hockey talent from government schools.
  • Delivering professional coaching and structured training.
  • Creating equal opportunities for boys and girls to participate.
  • Promoting fitness, discipline, teamwork, and leadership.
  • Providing competitive platforms for district, state, and national pathways.
  • Building sustainable partnerships with schools, communities, and CSR supporters.

Our Commitment

Every child we work with receives coaching, competitive opportunity, and mentorship, regardless of background. Non-negotiable.
