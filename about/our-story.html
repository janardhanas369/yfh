Who We Are

Born From a Field Visit

In 2019, a small group of educators and former hockey players visited a rural government school on the outskirts of Coimbatore. They expected to drop off donated books. They left with a different plan entirely. The children had never held a hockey stick — but their speed, their hunger to learn, and their raw talent were unmistakable.

Within months, that visit had become a registered NGO. Coaches volunteered weekends. Donors contributed sticks and shoes. Teachers stayed after school to tutor. What began as one school quickly became ten, then forty, then a movement spanning three states.

Today, more than 500 children train and learn with YES For Hockey — every one of them at zero cost to their families. The first stick we handed out is still in use.

What We Do

  • Academic tutoring in core subjects, every week.
  • Hockey coaching by certified, state-level coaches.
  • Free equipment — sticks, balls, shoes, kits.
  • Talent identification and competitive pathways.
  • Community building among children, families, and schools.

Our Commitment

Every program is completely FREE for the children. No child is ever charged for coaching, equipment, or support. Non-negotiable.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

Five years of small, stubborn steps — each one a turning point for the children we serve.

Year 1 · 2019

The First Visit

A field visit to a rural government school outside Coimbatore sparks the idea. Twenty children try a hockey stick for the first time on a dusty ground.

Year 2 · 2020

10 Schools Enrolled

Word spreads. Ten government schools sign on. The first batch of volunteer coaches is trained, and free equipment is distributed across Coimbatore district.

Year 3 · 2021

First State-Level Player

A YES For Hockey trainee is selected for the state hockey squad — the first proof that the pathway works. Local press takes notice.

Year 4 · 2022

Academic Wing Launched

We formally add free academic tutoring alongside coaching. Volunteer teachers join every weekend session to keep children thriving in school.

Year 5+ · 2023–Today

National Recognition

Three national honours, 500+ children enrolled, and a presence in Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Karnataka. The journey continues — one school at a time.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Six principles that guide every session, every decision, and every child we welcome.

Inclusivity

Every child is welcome — regardless of caste, gender, ability, or family income. No tryouts, no fees, no gatekeeping.

Excellence

We coach to national standards. Talent should not be capped by where a child happens to be born.

Integrity

Every rupee is accounted for. Every promise to a child is kept. We publish our reports and answer hard questions.

Compassion

We meet children where they are. A bad day, a missed session, a tough home life — coaching is patient, never punitive.

Perseverance

From dusty school grounds to national stages — we walk the long road with every child, season after season.

Community

Schools, families, donors, and volunteers move together. No child grows in isolation; we build villages around them.
