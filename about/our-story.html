Who We Are

Born on a Government School Ground

It started with a simple observation: government school children in Bengaluru had the speed, the hunger, and the discipline to play competitive hockey. What they lacked was access. No coach, no stick, no pathway.

YFHA was built to close that gap. We began with a handful of schools and a group of committed coaches. Today, we run structured hockey programs across 8+ government schools including KPS Jeevan Bheema Nagar, KPS Thanisandra, KPS Kodigehalli, KPS T Dasarahalli, KPS Devara Jeevanahalli, KPS Hegganahalli, KPS Agrahara, and KPS Bellandur.

Every session, every league match, and every selection to a district or state camp reflects the same founding belief: talent should never be limited by a child's zip code.

What We Do

  • Structured hockey coaching within government schools.
  • Fitness, skill, and performance assessment for every player.
  • Inter-school leagues and competitive tournaments.
  • Talent identification and pathways to district, state, and national opportunities.
  • Community engagement with schools, parents, and coaches.

Our Commitment

Every child we work with receives coaching, competitive opportunity, and mentorship, regardless of background. Non-negotiable.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

From one school to 8+ government schools across Bengaluru — each milestone a turning point for the children we serve.

Year 1

The First Schools

YFHA begins structured coaching in a small group of government schools in Bengaluru.

Year 2

Building the Coaching Core

Certified coaches are onboarded and training schedules formalised across partner schools.

Year 3

Inter-School League Launched

The first Inter-School Hockey League brings partner schools together in organised competition.

Year 4

Team of Excellence Formed

High-potential players are selected into an advanced weekend training program.

Today

8+ Government Schools

Hundreds of young athletes, and a clear pathway from school ground to state and national selection.
