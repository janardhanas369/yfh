Why Give

Why Your Support Matters

For many talented children, the dream of playing hockey is limited by access to coaching and equipment, not by ability. Your generosity removes that barrier.

Real Impact

Your Donation Creates Real Impact

Every contribution directly fuels the resources, training, and opportunities that turn potential into achievement.

Professional Coaching

Structured training from experienced coaches who mentor every child on and off the field.

Hockey Equipment

Sticks, balls, shin guards, and shoes distributed free to every child who joins the program.

Fitness & Development

Year-round fitness sessions, nutrition guidance, and holistic athletic development.

School Hockey Programs

Free, fully managed hockey programs running in government schools across Bengaluru.

Tournaments & Competitions

Registration, travel, and exposure at district, state, and national-level tournaments.

Pathways to Excellence

Clear routes from school grounds to district, state, and national selection.

Flexible Giving

Ways to Give

Choose the giving method that works for you — every option creates lasting impact for young athletes.

One-Time Donation

Monthly Giving

Corporate Giving

CSR-aligned partnerships with co-branded programs and full impact reporting.

In-Kind Contributions

Donate hockey equipment, gear, or professional services directly to the program.
