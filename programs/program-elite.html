Team of Excellence & Talent Pathway

When a player shows exceptional promise, we make sure nothing stands between them and the next level.

Overview

About This Program

The Team of Excellence brings together our most promising players from across partner schools for advanced weekend coaching, tactical training, and competitive preparation.

Talented players from government schools are too often forced to stop at the exact moment they show promise, simply because the next step is out of reach. YFHA closes that gap, guiding players through district trials, state tournaments, DYES selection, and SAI Sports Hostel trials.

100% Free

This program is completely free for every enrolled child — no fees, no charges, ever.

In Detail

Objectives, Features & Benefits

What we set out to achieve, how we deliver it, and the change it creates in a child's life.

Objectives

  • Prepare high-potential players for district and state trials
  • Provide advanced, weekend-intensive coaching
  • Guide players through DYES and SAI Sports Hostel selection processes
  • Support long-term athletic development toward national opportunities

Features

  • Weekend High Performance Training
  • Advanced tactical and position-specific coaching
  • Player monitoring and performance tracking
  • State tournament participation
  • Mentorship through selection trials

Benefits

  • A real, proven pathway from school ground to state and national selection
  • Advanced coaching without cost to the family
  • Structured trial preparation and mentorship
  • A model that inspires the next generation of players
