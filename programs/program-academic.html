Overview

About This Program

Our Academic Mentorship program pairs volunteer tutors with children from government schools for weekly one-on-one and small-group sessions. We believe that sport and education go hand in hand — a child who learns confidently on the field learns confidently in the classroom too.

Tutors focus on core subjects where children most need support, build study habits that last a lifetime, and open pathways to higher education that would otherwise be out of reach.

100% Free

This program is completely free for every enrolled child — no fees, no charges, ever.
