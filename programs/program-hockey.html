Overview

About This Program

Grassroots Hockey Training is the heart of YES For Hockey. Certified coaches run structured sessions at partner government schools, providing every piece of equipment a child needs — sticks, balls, shin guards, shoes, and jerseys — completely free.

For many children, this is their first encounter with organised sport. Our coaches meet them where they are, build fundamentals with patience, and create a safe after-school environment where talent is discovered, not bought.

100% Free

This program is completely free for every enrolled child — no fees, no charges, ever.

Objectives, Features & Benefits

What we set out to achieve, how we deliver it, and the change it creates in a child's life.

Objectives

  • Introduce hockey to children with no prior access
  • Build fundamental skills
  • Develop fitness, teamwork, discipline
  • Identify talent early for Elite Pathway

Features

  • Certified coaches 10+ yrs experience
  • Sessions 3–4 days/week
  • All equipment free
  • Age-appropriate curriculum (8–12, 13–15, 16–18)
  • Inter-school matches & tournaments

Benefits

  • A sport for life
  • Improved fitness & discipline
  • Safe after-school environment
  • Clear talent pipeline
