A hockey stick teaches more than technique. We use every session to build character that lasts well beyond the field.

Overview

About This Program

Hockey is our tool, but character is our outcome. Alongside technical coaching, YFHA builds a structured personal development track into every training session, focused on discipline, confidence, leadership, and teamwork.

Coaches actively mentor players on goal-setting, sportsmanship, and the kind of resilience that helps a child push through a tough match, and a tough day at school.

100% Free

This program is completely free for every enrolled child — no fees, no charges, ever.

In Detail

Objectives, Features & Benefits

What we set out to achieve, how we deliver it, and the change it creates in a child's life.

Objectives

  • Build discipline, confidence, and self-belief
  • Develop leadership and communication skills
  • Encourage sportsmanship and respect for the game
  • Support each player's personal growth beyond hockey

Features

  • Goal-setting and progress conversations with coaches
  • Leadership responsibilities within team settings
  • Sportsmanship and fair-play coaching
  • Encouragement toward continued schooling and attendance
  • Recognition for character, not just performance

Benefits

  • Greater confidence, on the field and off it
  • Stronger classroom discipline and attendance
  • Leadership experience within a team
  • A genuine sense of belonging and purpose
