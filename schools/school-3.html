Government High School, Sulur

A proud partner school of YES For Hockey, where children receive free hockey coaching and academic support every week.

About the School

Disciplined Drills in Sulur

Government High School, Sulur joined YES For Hockey in 2020. With 15 enrolled children, this centre is known for its disciplined drill routines and remarkably consistent weekly attendance — a credit to the school's PE staff and parent community.

Children here train on a well-maintained ground near the school and attend tutoring sessions in maths, science, and Tamil. Several players have already represented Sulur in inter-block tournaments and are being groomed for the next district trials.

Programs at This School

Academic Support

Weekly small-group tutoring in maths, science, and Tamil. Sessions are aligned with the school timetable so children never miss core classes.

Structured weekend sessions focused on fitness, basic skills, and match play. Free sticks, balls, and shoes for every enrolled child.
