Corporation School, Hebbal

A proud partner school of YES For Hockey, where children receive free hockey coaching and academic support every week.

About the School

Our Newest Urban Centre

Corporation School, Hebbal became a YES For Hockey partner in 2022. With 17 enrolled children from one of Bengaluru's most dynamic neighbourhoods, this centre benefits from strong volunteer support — corporate employees, college students, and parents all pitch in.

The school's compact ground is perfect for skill-based drills and small-sided games. Children attend tutoring sessions in maths, science, and Kannada on weekday evenings, with hockey coaching every weekend morning.

Programs at This School

Academic Support

Weekday evening tutoring in maths, science, and Kannada. Volunteer tutors include corporate employees from nearby tech parks and students from local colleges.

Hockey Training

Skill-focused weekend coaching on the school's compact ground. Free gear, fitness drills, and small-sided games; promising players feed into the Bengaluru district trial pathway.
