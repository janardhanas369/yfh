Government School, Mysuru South

A proud partner school of YES For Hockey, where children receive free hockey coaching and academic support every week.

About the School

A Fast-Growing Centre

Government School, Mysuru South became a YES For Hockey partner in 2022. Despite being a relatively young centre, 19 enrolled children have made rapid progress — two players have already been called up to Karnataka state trials.

The school's large playground and supportive headmistress have made it possible to run full-field drills and small-sided matches. Tutoring sessions in maths, science, and Kannada run twice weekly, with strong volunteer support from Mysuru University students.

Academic Support
