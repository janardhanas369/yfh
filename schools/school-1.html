Government Higher Primary School, Coimbatore

A proud partner school of YES For Hockey, where children receive free hockey coaching and academic support every week.

About the School

Our First Partnership

Government Higher Primary School, Coimbatore became YES For Hockey's first partner school in 2019. What started as a small weekend coaching session for a handful of curious children has grown into a thriving weekly program with 18 enrolled players.

The school's headmaster and physical-education teacher work closely with our coaches to balance academics and sport. Children attend tutoring sessions in maths, science, and language during the week and pick up hockey sticks on weekends — completely free of cost.

Programs at This School

Academic Support

Free weekly tutoring in maths, science, and language. Volunteer tutors work with small groups of children after school hours, helping them keep pace with the curriculum.

Hockey Training

Certified coaches run weekend sessions with free sticks, balls, and shoes. Children learn dribbling, passing, tackling, and match play — building toward inter-school and district trials.

Outcomes

Achievements

  • 3 children selected for Coimbatore district hockey trials in 2023.
  • Improved academic pass rate — 92% of YFH children passed their grade this year.
  • Inter-school tournament finalists — Coimbatore Zonal Hockey, 2023.
  • Active weekly attendance of 90%+ across both hockey and academic sessions.
