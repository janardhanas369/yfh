Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know before joining YES For Hockey as a volunteer, partner, or donor.

Do I need a formal qualification to volunteer?
No formal qualification is required for most roles — what we look for is patience, enthusiasm, and a genuine interest in working with children. For specialised coaching roles, prior playing or coaching experience helps, but our team will train and onboard you regardless of background.
How many hours a week is expected?
There is no fixed minimum. Most volunteers give between 2 and 6 hours a week, but we have roles that fit anything from a one-off Saturday event to a steady weekly commitment. You tell us your availability and we match you to a school and slot that works.
Can volunteering be remote?
Yes. Tutoring, content creation, social media, fundraising, and design roles can be done remotely. On-ground coaching and event roles require your presence at a partner school, but we'll always tell you the location before you commit.
Can companies partner for CSR?
Absolutely. We work with corporates on CSR-aligned programs — equipment drives, sponsored tournaments, employee volunteering days, and full program sponsorships. All partnerships come with detailed impact reporting and 80G receipts. Reach out through the Corporate Partner enquiry form to begin.
How are donations used?
100% of your donation goes to programs — no admin bloat. Funds are spent on equipment (sticks, balls, shin guards, shoes), coaching stipends, academic materials, and tournament travel for selected players. You'll receive a receipt and, on request, a breakdown of how your contribution was used.
