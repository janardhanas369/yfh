Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know before joining YES For Hockey as a volunteer, partner, or donor.

Do I need hockey experience to volunteer?
No. While coaching volunteers benefit from playing or coaching experience, many roles exist in event management, communications, photography, and administration.
How many hours a week is expected?
There is no fixed minimum. Most volunteers give a few hours a week; some help with occasional events. Tell us your availability and we will match you accordingly.
Can I volunteer remotely?
Yes. Content creation, social media, design, and fundraising roles can be done remotely. Coaching and event roles require on-ground presence at a partner school.
Can companies partner for CSR?
Absolutely. We work with corporates on CSR-aligned programs, equipment drives, sponsored tournaments, and full impact reporting.
How are donations used?
Funds go directly toward coaching, equipment, and tournament participation for the children we serve. Donors receive updates on how their contribution made an impact.
