Empowering Every Child. Inspiring Every Dream.

Yes For Hockey! Association is a grassroots movement discovering, coaching, and championing young hockey talent in government schools across Bengaluru. One stick, one school, one dream at a time.

Trusted by Schools. Driven by Purpose. We partner with government schools, coaches, and communities to turn hockey into a catalyst for confidence, discipline, and opportunity.

Why Hockey

Building Champions from the Ground Up

We do not just teach hockey. We shape character, resilience, teamwork, and leadership. Our structured programs give every child from a government school the chance to learn, grow, compete, and excel. Whether a child is picking up a stick for the first time or dreaming of the national jersey, YFHA provides the ground, the coach, and the pathway.

Professional Coaching

Qualified coaches delivering structured, age-appropriate training.

Equal Opportunity

Inclusive programs for boys and girls from government schools.

Long-Term Development

Consistent progress over quick wins, from school ground to state level.
